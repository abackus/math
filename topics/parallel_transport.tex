Let $E$ be a tensor bundle over $M$, let $T^\vol M$ be the volume bundle $T^\vol M = (T')^{\wedge d} M$, and let $U$ be a star based at $P$.
Then there is a well-defined parallel transport map $\Trans_{E, P}: E_U \to E_P$, which maps a tensor $v$ at $x \in U$ to a tensor at $P$ by transporting $v$ along the unique geodesic from $x$ to $P$.
Moreover, if $V$ is a separable Hilbert space then integration is a well-defined map $\int: V \otimes C^\infty(M, T^\vol M) \to V$, thus we can define for a tensor field $X$,
$$\int_U \star 1 \otimes \Trans_{E, P} X \in E_P.$$

\begin{lemma}
Let $(U_n)$ be sets of least perimeter meeting the hypotheses of the mollification result, and let $(V_n)$ be their mollifications.
Then 
$$\left|\int_{tB_n} \star 1 \otimes \Trans_{E, P} \dif (1_{U_n} - 1_{V_n})\right| \ll \gamma_n \Delta_n^{d - 1}.$$
\end{lemma}
